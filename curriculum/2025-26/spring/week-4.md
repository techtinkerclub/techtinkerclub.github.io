## Week 4 — Duplicate, Patterns & Basic Mirror

**Focus Concept:** Repetition & symmetry  
**Mini-Project:** *Symmetric Creature or Rocket*

<div class="notice--steam">
  <h2>Connections to STEAM Learning</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Computing:</strong> Repetition concepts in design.</li>
    <li><strong>Design & Technology:</strong> Planning mirrored parts.</li>
    <li><strong>Maths:</strong> Lines of symmetry.</li>
    <li><strong>Art & Design:</strong> Pattern and balance.</li>
    <li><strong>Engineering:</strong> Structural balance.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

Learners explore duplication and mirroring to create balanced, appealing designs.

---

## Objectives
- Use duplicate to repeat shapes.  
- Mirror features accurately.

---

## Success Criteria
- I can duplicate shapes quickly.  
- I can mirror shapes to make a balanced model.

---

## Key Vocabulary
- **Duplicate** — copy shapes.  
- **Mirror** — flip shapes.  
- **Axis** — direction of flipping.

---

## Part A — Explore Duplicate
Repeat shapes for patterns.

## Part B — Mirror Tools
Create symmetry in designs.

## Part C — Extend
Decorate creature or rocket.
