## Week 2 — Align & Group

**Focus Concept:** Shape arrangement  
**Mini-Project:** *Simple Character*

<div class="notice--steam">
  <h2>Connections to STEAM Learning</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Computing:</strong> Precision placement.</li>
    <li><strong>Design & Technology:</strong> Constructing simple products.</li>
    <li><strong>Maths:</strong> Symmetry and spacing.</li>
    <li><strong>Art & Design:</strong> Character design.</li>
    <li><strong>Engineering:</strong> Thinking about balance.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

Use align and group to build a simple face or creature with correctly placed features.

---

## Objectives
- Use align to position shapes.  
- Group shapes into a single model.  

## Success Criteria
- I can use the align tool.  
- I can build a simple character.

## Key Vocabulary
- **Align** — lining up shapes.  
- **Group** — joining shapes.

---

## Part A — Explore Align
Align eyes, mouth and features.

## Part B — Build Character
Add head, eyes, mouth, decorations.

## Part C — Extend
Add hat, ears, accessories.

---

## Resources

## Equipment
- Laptops, mice.

## Safety
Avoid tiny decorations.
