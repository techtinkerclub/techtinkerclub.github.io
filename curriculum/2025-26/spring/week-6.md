## Week 6 — Design a Simple Car Body

**Focus Concept:** Combining tools to form a single functional body  
**Mini-Project:** *Car Body Prototype*

Learners build a simple car body, preparing for next week’s motion work.

---

## Objectives
- Combine Week 1–5 tools into a single model.  
- Design a body that wheels can attach to.  
- Use symmetry to keep shape balanced.

## Success Criteria
- I can design a solid body.  
- I can create wheel arches or placeholders.  
- I can keep the model balanced.

## Key Vocabulary
- **Chassis** — base frame of a vehicle.  
- **Symmetry** — balanced layout.  
- **Clearance** — space needed for movement.

---

## Part A — Recap Tools
Align, Group, Holes, Duplicate, Sketch (optional).

## Part B — Build Car Body
Form the main chassis & wheel arches.

## Part C — Extend
Add windows or decorations.
