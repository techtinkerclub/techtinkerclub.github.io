## Week 3 — Holes & Negative Space

**Focus Concept:** Cutting shapes using holes  
**Mini-Project:** *Simple Stamp / Cutter*

<div class="notice--steam">
  <h2>Connections to STEAM Learning</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Computing:</strong> Using Boolean geometry (add/remove).</li>
    <li><strong>Design & Technology:</strong> Designing functional shapes.</li>
    <li><strong>Maths:</strong> Estimating depth and size.</li>
    <li><strong>Art & Design:</strong> Designing icons and motifs.</li>
    <li><strong>Engineering:</strong> Understanding clearances.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

This week learners explore the hole tool to remove material and create cut‑outs.

---

## Objectives
- Use holes to remove shapes.  
- Apply negative space to create a simple stamp.

---

## Success Criteria
- I can turn shapes into holes.  
- I can cut shapes cleanly from solids.

---

## Key Vocabulary
- **Hole** — a shape that removes material.  
- **Negative Space** — empty space that forms a shape.  
- **Clearance** — space around a feature.

---

## Part A — Explore Hole Tool
Turn shapes into holes and group them.

## Part B — Make Stamp
Cut out a symbol or letter.

## Part C — Extend
Add frame or second cutout.

---

- Laptops and mice.

## Safety
Avoid thin edges.
