# Instructor Notes — Week 1
{% include print-to-pdf.html %}

**Theme:** 3D Basics  
**Focus Concept:** Navigation & basic shapes  
**Mini-Project:** *Name Tag / Keyring*

---

## Learning Objectives
- Navigate 3D space.  
- Use basic shape tools.  

---

## Session Flow (≈ 80 min)
| Segment | Time | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Welcome | 10 | Intro Tinkercad |
| Demo | 10 | Orbit, pan, zoom |
| Guided | 25 | Build base + letters |
| Independent | 25 | Decorate & refine |
| Share | 10 | Gallery |

---

## Part A — Demo
Show navigation and shapes.

## Part B — Guided
Build a base + initials.

## Part C — Independent
Add decoration or loop.

---

## Vocabulary
Orbit, Pan, Zoom, Group.

## Instructor Tips
Keep pace slow; navigation is hardest.

## Assessment
Check movement and resizing skills.

## Misconceptions
“Zoom = move model.” Clarify camera.

## Differentiation
Beginners: 2 letters. Confident: full name.

## Cross-Curricular
Maths (size), D&T (purpose), Art (design).

## KS2 Mapping
Creating media; D&T design cycle.

## Materials
Laptops, mice.

## Safety
No tiny shapes for printing.
