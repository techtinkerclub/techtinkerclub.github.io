# Instructor Notes — Week 4
{% include print-to-pdf.html %}

**Theme:** Duplicate & Mirror  
**Focus Concept:** Repetition & symmetry  
**Mini-Project:** *Symmetric Creature or Rocket*

---

## Learning Objectives
- Mirror and duplicate shapes correctly.

---

## Session Flow (≈ 80 min)
| Segment | Time | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Recap | 10 | Holes |
| Demo | 10 | Duplicate & Mirror |
| Guided | 25 | Base creature |
| Independent | 25 | Add symmetry |
| Share | 10 | Gallery |

---

## Part A
Show duplicate and rotation.

## Part B
Demonstrate mirroring.

## Part C
Independent symmetry.

---

## Vocabulary
Duplicate, Mirror, Axis.

## Tips
Keep centre line clear.

## Assessment
Check symmetry accuracy.

## Misconceptions
“Mirror moves shapes randomly.” Show anchor.

## Differentiation
Beginners: two-part creature.  
Confident: complex rocket.

## Cross Curricular
Art & symmetry.

## KS2 Mapping
Computing patterns; Maths symmetry.

## Materials
Laptops, mice.

## Safety
Monitor screen use.
