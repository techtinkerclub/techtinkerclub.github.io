# Instructor Notes — Week 5
{% include print-to-pdf.html %}

**Theme:** Sketch + Revolve  
**Focus Concept:** Turning profiles into 3D forms  
**Mini-Project:** *Rocket Bottle / Vase / Cup*

---

## Learning Objectives
- Use Sketch tool correctly.  
- Create clean profile for Revolve.

## Session Flow (≈ 80 min)
| Segment | Time | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Recap | 10 | Duplicate & Mirror |
| Demo | 15 | Sketch + Revolve |
| Guided | 25 | Create profile + revolve |
| Independent | 20 | Decorations |
| Share | 10 | Review & save |

---

## Guidance
Show how closed profiles matter.  
Demonstrate axis selection.

## Vocabulary
Sketch, Profile, Revolve, Axis.

## Assessment
Check clean revolve & thickness.

## Misconceptions
“Any scribble works.” Explain closed shapes.

## Differentiation
Beginners: cup. Confident: rocket.

## KS2 Mapping
D&T technical knowledge; Maths rotational symmetry.
