# Instructor Notes — Week 3
{% include print-to-pdf.html %}

**Theme:** Holes & Negative Space  
**Focus Concept:** Cutting material  
**Mini-Project:** *Stamp / Cutter*

---

## Learning Objectives
- Use hole and group together.  
- Create clean cutouts.

---

## Session Flow (≈ 80 min)
| Segment | Time | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Recap | 10 | Align & Group |
| Demo | 10 | Hole tool |
| Guided | 25 | Cutout design |
| Independent | 25 | Extend |
| Share | 10 | Review work |

---

## Part A
Show hole tool clearly.

## Part B
Guide a simple cutout.

## Part C
Encourage creativity.

---

## Vocabulary
Hole, Negative Space, Clearance.

## Tips
Check grouping order.

## Assessment
Evaluate clean cuts.

## Misconceptions
“Holes print white.” Clarify removal.

## Differentiation
Beginners: simple letter.  
Confident: icon or symbol.

## Cross Curricular
Art (icons), Maths (depth).

## KS2 Mapping
D&T making skills.

## Materials
Laptops, mice.

## Safety
Avoid sharp thin forms.
