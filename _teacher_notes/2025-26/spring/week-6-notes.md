# Instructor Notes — Week 6
{% include print-to-pdf.html %}

**Theme:** Car Body Design  
**Focus Concept:** Using multiple tools for one coherent model  
**Mini-Project:** *Car Body Prototype*

---

## Learning Objectives
- Use multi-tool workflow.  
- Achieve symmetry & clearance.

## Session Flow (≈ 80 min)
| Segment | Time | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Recap | 10 | Holes + Mirror |
| Demo | 10 | Chassis basics |
| Guided | 25 | Build wheel arches |
| Independent | 25 | Body shape |
| Share | 10 | Review |

---

## Guidance
Use duplicate+mirror for wheel arches.  
Stress clearance for wheel fit.

## Vocabulary
Chassis, Clearance, Symmetry.

## Assessment
Check wheel spacing & stability.

## Misconceptions
“All wheels fit anywhere.” Explain clearance.

## Differentiation
Beginners: simple arch.  
Confident: stylised shell.

## KS2 Mapping
D&T design cycle; Engineering concepts.
